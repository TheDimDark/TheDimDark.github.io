gato

This is my senior project, in which I worked on alongside a partner. I primarily covered the front-end programming and the database queries, but also handled the majority of written documentation. Word Guess Game

HTML5, CSS3, Javascript

My Client-Side course required use to build a word guessing game in order for us to demonstrate our ability to utilize Javascript. This webpage selects one word from a predetermined key of words, and scrambles it. The user gets three chances to guess the word, and is rewarded a number of points and starts based off of how quickly they guess the word. Each level increases the length of the scrambled word by one character in order to provide a form of scaling difficulty for the user. Positive and negative feedback is provided by sounds as well as visual graphics and text.
